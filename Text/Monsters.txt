属性は水。氷雪、氷河期にまつわる災禍を\n具現化した厄災。氷雪の階層に生息し、\n雪の中の地中を移動する。\n大穴内の階層に出現した時から、\n大穴の周辺や前線基地に至るまで\n異常気象を発生させ、季節外れの大雪や、\n氷の世界をもたらす。=Attribute: Water. A Calamity that materializes disasters related to ice, snow, and glacial eras. It inhabits the ice and snow strata, moving through the ground beneath the snow. Since its appearance in the strata of the Abyss, it has caused abnormal weather as far as the Abyss's surroundings and frontline bases, bringing unseasonable blizzards and a world of ice.
属性は闇。飢えと枯れにまつわる災禍を\n具現化した厄災。周囲の植物を枯らし、\n生物を飢えさせて死に至らしめ、\n凶作と飢餓をもたらす。\n大穴出現直後に最初に出現した厄災であり\n連合調査隊及び、大穴周辺に甚大な被害を\n与えた。三大国の協力によって\n一度は退けることに成功するも、\n大穴内のある階層で力を蓄えた後、\n幾度も人類の前にその脅威をもたらす。=Attribute: Darkness. A Calamity that materializes disasters related to hunger and blight. It withers surrounding plants, starves living creatures to death, and brings crop failure and famine. It was the first Calamity to appear immediately after the Abyss emerged, inflicting massive damage on the Allied Expedition and the areas around the Abyss. Though the Three Nations succeeded in repelling it once through their cooperation, it later amassed power in a certain stratum of the Abyss and has repeatedly brought its threat before humanity.
